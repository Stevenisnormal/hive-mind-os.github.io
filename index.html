Intro

Hive Mind OS is a revolutionary extension for your bee hive. Once you attach Hive Mind OS to your bee hive you can see live data from your hive right on your smart phone. You can also seal your hive from wherever you are so if their is a pesticide spray near your hive and you are not around you can seal your hive straight from your smartphone. This could help to save thousands of dollars worth of precious bees and honey, and keep you knowing that your hives are safe.

Design

Our design uses a Particle Electron, a small, cellular-connected board that allows Hive Mind OS to connect to the cloud and report sensor data form the board to a simple mobile app that will allow beekeepers to view statistics and close their bee hive right from their smart phone. This solution allows beekeepers to be easily notified of any urgent issues and monitor honey production. Hive Mind OS also allows beekeepers to seal their hives if there is a chemical spray near their hives.

Components

  • Particle Electron - The main board of the product which connects it to the app via a cellular service.
  • Weight Sensor - Monitor how much honey your bees are producing remotely.
  • Servo Door (x2) - Used to seal up the hive during pesticide sprays.
  • Temperature Sensor - Makes sure that the bees are safe while the hive is closed.
